Q: Co nhung cach nao de tao 1 project Laravel?
A: Co nhung cach sau de tao 1 project Laravel:
1. Su dung Composer:
 - Cai dat Composer
 - Chay cau lenh "composer create-project --prefer-dist laravel/laravel project_name" de tao project 
2. Su dung Laravel Installer va Composer
 - Cai dat Composer
 - Cai dat Laravel: composer global require laravel/installer
 - Cai dat project bang Laravel Installer: laravel new project_name
